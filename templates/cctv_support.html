Common CCTV Problem: Camera Not Recording

Problem: Security camera appears to be working but is not recording footage to the DVR/NVR.

Solution:

  1. Check storage space on the DVR/NVR and clear old recordings if storage is full
  2. Verify recording schedule settings and ensure cameras are set to record continuously or on motion
  3. Check camera connections and ensure all cables are securely connected
  4. Test camera functionality by viewing live feed and checking for any error messages
  5. Restart the DVR/NVR system and check if recording resumes after reboot

Common CCTV Problem: Poor Image Quality

Problem: CCTV cameras are producing blurry, dark, or low-quality images.

Solution:

  1. Clean camera lenses with appropriate cleaning materials to remove dirt and smudges
  2. Adjust camera focus and zoom settings for optimal image clarity
  3. Check lighting conditions and install additional lighting if needed for night vision
  4. Verify camera resolution settings and ensure they are set to maximum quality
  5. Check for interference from other electronic devices and relocate if necessary

Common CCTV Problem: Remote Access Not Working

Problem: Unable to view CCTV cameras remotely through mobile app or web browser.

Solution:

  1. Check internet connection on both the DVR/NVR and the viewing device
  2. Verify port forwarding settings on the router for the CCTV system
  3. Update mobile app or web browser to the latest version
  4. Check firewall settings and ensure CCTV ports are not blocked
  5. Reset network settings on the DVR/NVR and reconfigure remote access

Common CCTV Problem: Motion Detection Not Working

Problem: Motion detection alerts are not triggering when movement occurs in camera view.

Solution:

  1. Check motion detection sensitivity settings and adjust to appropriate level
  2. Verify motion detection zones are properly configured for areas of interest
  3. Ensure motion detection is enabled in the camera or DVR/NVR settings
  4. Check for obstructions or environmental factors affecting motion detection
  5. Test motion detection by walking in front of the camera during different times

Common CCTV Problem: System Keeps Rebooting

Problem: DVR/NVR system randomly restarts or shuts down without warning.

Solution:

  1. Check power supply and ensure it provides adequate wattage for all connected cameras
  2. Verify all connections are secure and not causing power fluctuations
  3. Check system temperature and ensure proper ventilation around the DVR/NVR
  4. Update firmware to the latest version to fix known stability issues
  5. Test with fewer cameras connected to isolate potential hardware conflicts
