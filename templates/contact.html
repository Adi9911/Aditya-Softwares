How quickly can you respond to technical support requests?

We typically respond to all support requests within 24 hours during business days. For emergency issues, we offer same-day response and can provide immediate assistance for critical problems.

Do you provide on-site technical support?

Yes, we offer on-site support for complex hardware issues, network setup, and situations that require physical presence. On-site services are available by appointment and may include additional charges based on location and complexity.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, online payments, and cash for on-site services. Payment terms are discussed during project consultation and clearly outlined in our service agreements.

Do you offer warranties on your services?

Yes, we provide warranties on all our services. Hardware repairs come with parts and labor warranty, software development includes bug fixes and minor modifications, and technical support includes follow-up assistance to ensure issues are fully resolved.

Can you help with both Windows and Mac systems?

Absolutely! Our technicians are experienced with Windows, macOS, and Linux systems. We provide comprehensive support for all major operating systems and can help with cross-platform compatibility issues.
